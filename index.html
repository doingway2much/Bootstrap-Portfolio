About Me

My name is Seth Randell and I am originally from Los Angeles, California. I moved to Atlanta shortly after my journey into IT began over 20 years ago. I started out working tech support for one of the largest internet service providers. I moved my way up over the next 15 years to a Senior Systems Administrator position. After I left I took my love for virtualization and found a home at a small public cloud provider based in Atlanta. That’s where I found my true love for virtualization but I wanted more. This is what got me interested in coding so I could fill some of the gaps that I was missing in my tool kit. I am currently attending Georgia Tech’s Coding Boot Camp and will be working on some ingesting projects that I will be documenting here. So stay tuned and check back to see what I am up too.
